FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about LoanLeaf services and processes.

Applying is simple! Just click on the "Apply Now" button on our website and complete the online application form. You'll need to provide some basic personal and financial information. The entire process takes about 10 minutes, and you'll receive a preliminary decision immediately.

To be eligible for a LoanLeaf personal loan, you must: be at least 18 years old, have a valid government-issued ID, provide proof of income, have an active bank account, and meet our credit requirements. Specific requirements may vary based on loan amount and term.

Most applicants receive an instant preliminary decision. Final approval typically takes 1-2 business days after we've verified all your information. Once approved, funds are usually deposited into your account within 1 business day.

Interest rates range from 5% to 15% APR depending on your creditworthiness, loan amount, and repayment term. We offer fixed-rate loans, so your rate won't change over the life of your loan.

No, there are absolutely no prepayment penalties with LoanLeaf. You can pay off your loan early at any time without additional fees, and you'll save on interest by doing so.

We offer several convenient payment options: automatic bank transfers (ACH), online payments through your LoanLeaf account, debit card payments, or checks by mail. You can also set up autopay to ensure you never miss a payment.

Still have questions? Contact our support team for assistance.
